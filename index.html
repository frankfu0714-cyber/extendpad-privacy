Privacy Policy — ExtendPad

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Zero data collected

ExtendPad does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data — ever. All communication stays entirely on your local Wi-Fi network between your iPad and your PC.

1

Information We Collect

ExtendPad collects no personal information whatsoever. The App operates entirely on your local network. No data is sent to external servers, cloud services, or third parties of any kind.

2

Local Network Usage

The App requires access to your local network solely to:

Discover and connect to the ExtendPad companion app running on your PC
Stream your PC screen to your iPad over Wi-Fi in real time
Forward touch input from your iPad back to your PC

No network traffic leaves your local Wi-Fi network. The App never contacts any remote server, analytics service, or advertising platform.

3

Screen Content

Screen content streamed from your PC to your iPad is transmitted in real time over your local network and is never stored, logged, or uploaded anywhere. It exists only in memory during an active streaming session and is discarded immediately when the session ends.

4

Crash Reports & Analytics

The App does not include any crash reporting SDK, analytics SDK, or third-party tracking library. No usage data, telemetry, or diagnostic information is collected.

5

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from any user, including children under the age of 13. It is fully compliant with COPPA and applicable children's privacy laws.

6

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since no data is collected, substantive changes are unlikely.
