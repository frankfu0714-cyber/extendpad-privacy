Privacy Policy — ExtendPad

Last updated: March 15, 2026

ExtendPad ("the App") is developed by Yuan-Cheng Fu ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

ExtendPad does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The App operates entirely on your local Wi-Fi network. All communication happens directly between your iPad and your personal computer — no data is sent to external servers, cloud services, or third parties of any kind.

2. Local Network Usage

The App requires access to your local network solely to:

  • Discover and connect to the ExtendPad companion app running on your PC
  • Stream your PC screen to your iPad over Wi-Fi
  • Forward touch input from your iPad back to your PC

No network traffic leaves your local network. The App does not contact any remote server, analytics service, or advertising platform.

3. Screen Content

Screen content streamed from your PC to your iPad is transmitted in real time over your local network and is never stored, logged, or uploaded anywhere. It exists only in memory during an active streaming session.

4. Crash Reports & Analytics

The App does not include any crash reporting SDK, analytics SDK, or third-party tracking library. No usage data is collected.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from any user, including children under the age of 13. It is fully compliant with COPPA.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Since no data is collected, substantive changes are unlikely.
